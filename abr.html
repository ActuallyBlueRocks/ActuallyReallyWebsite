So I added the word blue to it, and now its stuck with me forever.

AFPP

AFPP, also know as Alans Fast Paced Place, is a baldi fangame/mod.

Originally the mod had been based off a fnf song called "Bruhing!".

And with some backlash from the actual creators of faldi, faldi was changed.

Thus alan was born.

UDABM

Untitled Dave And Bambi Mod is a fnf mod created by the one and only SoapyDX.

UDABM is a compilation mod based off the dave and bambi community.

Me and sabel (soapyDX) both put tons of time into the mod and we'd appreciate it if you downloaded it.

Bart FNF mod Simpson

Bart FNF mod Simpson is a shitpost based off a unity game my friend sabel made.

In the unity game there was a random chance for bart to show up, play a sine wave sound, and say a random clip

from the simpsons hit and run game.

So I turned that into a fnf mod.
